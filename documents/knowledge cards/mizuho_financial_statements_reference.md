# Mizuho FY2025 Financial Statements -- Deep Reference

## Purpose

Reference document summarising consolidated financial performance,
balance sheet, business segments and management commentary.

## Financial Highlights

-   Ordinary income: ¥9.085T
-   Ordinary profit: ¥1.573T (+34.6%)
-   Profit attributable to owners: ¥1.249T (+41%)
-   Total assets: ¥302.2T
-   Net assets: ¥11.4T
-   FY2026 profit guidance: ¥1.30T

## Management Commentary

Management attributes improved performance to: - Strong fee business
growth in Japan and overseas. - Higher Bank of Japan policy rates. - Yen
depreciation. - Continued expense discipline while investing in
governance and growth. - Higher forward-looking credit provisions
reflecting geopolitical uncertainty.

## Balance Sheet

Major assets: - Loans: ¥99.8T - Securities: ¥42.6T - Trading assets:
¥30.5T

Major liabilities: - Deposits: ¥165.9T - Bonds & Notes: ¥15.4T

## Cash Flow

Operating and investing cash flows were negative due to trading assets
and securities activity, while financing reflected subordinated debt
redemption.
