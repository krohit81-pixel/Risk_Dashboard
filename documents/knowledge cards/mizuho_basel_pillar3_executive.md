# Mizuho Basel Pillar 3 -- Executive Knowledge Card

## Purpose

Summarises Mizuho Financial Group's regulatory capital, liquidity,
leverage and risk profile under Basel III. This document is intended as
the primary retrieval source for executive intelligence.

## Executive Summary

Mizuho reports strong capital and liquidity buffers, with CET1, Tier 1
and Total Capital ratios comfortably above regulatory minimums. The
disclosure highlights capital strength, liquidity resilience,
diversified risk management and a balance sheet able to support global
banking activities.

## Executive Dashboard (30 September 2025)

  Metric                                Value
  -------------------------- ----------------
  CET1 Ratio                           13.70%
  Tier 1 Ratio                         16.55%
  Total Capital Ratio                  18.41%
  Leverage Ratio                        5.07%
  Liquidity Coverage Ratio             132.2%
  Net Stable Funding Ratio             115.0%
  Risk Weighted Assets         ¥74.2 trillion

## What this tells us about Mizuho

-   Capital preservation remains a strategic priority.
-   Liquidity is maintained comfortably above regulatory minimums.
-   Credit risk is the largest contributor to RWA.
-   Counterparty, market and operational risks remain material capital
    consumers.
-   Funding stability and leverage discipline remain central management
    themes.
