# Mizuho Business Model\
\
## Executive Summary\
\
Mizuho operates a diversified universal banking model combining\
domestic retail banking, corporate banking, trust banking,\
asset management and a growing Global Corporate &\
Investment Banking franchise.\
\
The objective of this knowledge card is to enable\
Mizuho Lens to map external events directly\
to affected businesses.\
