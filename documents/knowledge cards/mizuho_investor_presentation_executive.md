# Mizuho FY2025 Investor Presentation -- Executive Knowledge Card

## Purpose

Executive summary of management strategy, outlook and investment
narrative.

## Executive Summary

The presentation highlights record FY25 earnings, a raised medium-term
ambition, confidence in Japan's structural revival, disciplined risk
management, and continued investment in focus business areas. Management
targets sustainable ROE above 12% by FY28 while balancing growth and
shareholder returns.
