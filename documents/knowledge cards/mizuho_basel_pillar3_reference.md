## Capital Structure

### CET1

Primary loss absorbing capital.

### Additional Tier 1

Hybrid capital instruments supporting going-concern resilience.

### Tier 2

Supplementary capital supporting gone-concern protection.

## Risk Weighted Assets

Largest contributors include: 1. Credit Risk 2. Operational Risk 3.
Market Risk 4. Counterparty Credit Risk 5. CVA Risk 6. Securitisation

## Credit Risk

Uses a combination of Standardised and Internal Ratings Based (IRB)
approaches across sovereign, bank, corporate, retail and specialised
lending portfolios.

## Counterparty Credit Risk

Includes SA-CCR, Expected Positive Exposure and Central Counterparty
exposures.

## Market Risk

Primarily managed under the Standardised Approach.

## Operational Risk

Material contributor to regulatory capital and reflects operational
resilience requirements.

## Liquidity

Liquidity is monitored using: - Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR) - Net
Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR) - High Quality Liquid Assets (HQLA)

## Leverage

Leverage ratio complements risk-based capital measures by constraining
total exposure growth.

## TLAC

Maintains sufficient Total Loss Absorbing Capacity to support orderly
resolution.
