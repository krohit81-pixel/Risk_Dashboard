# Mizuho FY2025 Financial Statements -- Executive Knowledge Card

## Purpose

Executive summary of Mizuho Financial Group's FY2025 consolidated
financial results under Japanese GAAP.

## Executive Summary

FY2025 was a strong earnings year driven by fee income growth, yen
depreciation and higher Bank of Japan policy rates. Ordinary profit and
net profit reached record levels while total assets exceeded ¥302
