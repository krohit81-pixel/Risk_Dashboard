# Mizuho FY2025 Investor Presentation -- Deep Reference

## Strategic Narrative

Management believes Japan has exited its prolonged low-growth
environment, supported by higher inflation, wage growth, BOJ
normalization, increasing corporate investment, overseas investor
interest and retail migration from savings into investments.

## Financial Ambition

-   FY25 profit attributable to owners: JPY 1,248.6B
-   FY26 guidance: JPY 1,300B
-   FY28 ROE target: \>12%
-   Target consolidated net business profits: JPY 1.8--2.0T
-   Continue improving valuation toward P/B ratio above current 1.49.

## Business Strategy

Growth is centered around: - Global Corporate & Investment Banking -
Wealth & Asset Management - Japanese retail banking - Supporting
Japanese corporates globally - Sustainability and innovation financing

## Risk Management Philosophy

Management emphasizes: - Disciplined financial management - Sound
portfolio construction - Forward-looking provisioning - Flexibility
during geopolitical uncertainty - Conservative risk appetite -
Customer-flow focused markets business

## Executive Intelligence Mapping

Useful for analysing: - Japan interest-rate changes - BOJ policy -
Corporate financing - Equity valuation - Share buybacks - Credit
quality - Geopolitical developments - Capital markets
